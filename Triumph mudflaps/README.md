# 🏍️ Triumph Speed / Scrambler 400 Mudflap
A functional, aesthetic solution to the excessive "mud-sling" on the Triumph 400 series. Designed to sit flush with the factory fender for a clean, OEM+ look.



### 🛠️ Specifications
* **Compatibility:** Triumph Speed 400, Scrambler 400x, Speed T4, Thruxton 400.
* **Material:** Elegoo TPU 95A (Required for flexibility and impact resistance).
* **Variants:** * `SPEED 400.step`: Standard length.
    * `SCRAMBLER 400X.step`: +2cm length to account for longer suspension travel.

### 🔧 Installation Guide
1.  **Mounting:** Position the flap between the fender Nut and the Washer.
    **Sequence:** Nut -> **Mudflap** -> Washer.
2.  **Adhesion:** Use 3M VHB double-sided tape on the bottom contact point to prevent flapping at high speeds.
3.  **Tightening:** Hold the flap with your thumb while tightening the nut to ensure it stays flush and doesn't rotate or over stretch.

### 💡 Print Settings (Recommended)
* **Wall Loops:** 3-4
* **Infill:** 15-20% (Gyroid)
* **Support:** Not required.
