# Dragon Keys
> **Handcrafted. Open Source. Built for enthusiasts.**

Dragon Keys is a Chennai-based design house and workshop specializing in ergonomic mechanical keyboards, precision 3D-printed automotive accessories, and custom maker tools. 

We believe that the best tools are the ones you can understand, repair, and modify. That’s why our designs are open-sourced for the community to build, learn, and iterate.

### 🛠️ Our Philosophy
* **Precision Craft:** Every physical unit sold is hand-assembled and tested.
* **Open Access:** CAD files and firmware are available here for personal use.
* **Community First:** Designed for those who appreciate the click of a well-lubed switch and the fit of a perfectly engineered part.

### 📂 Repository Directory
* [**Dragonfly 67**](./Dragonfly 67 keyboard/): 67-key ergonomic split mechanical keyboard.
* [**Triumph 400 Series Mudflap**](./Triumph mudflaps/): Flexible TPU rear protection for Speed & Scrambler.
